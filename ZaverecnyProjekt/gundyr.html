Introduction

 Iudex Gundyr is a Boss in Dark Souls 3. He is the first Boss that players will encounter. The player will have to face him in order to gain access to Firelink Shrine.

 General info:

  • Health: 1037
  • Souls dropped after death: 3000
  • Recommended Levels: Any
  • Drops: Coiled Sword

 Weaknesses/Strenghts/Immunities:

  • Weak against: Fire, Lightning, Frost
  • Resistant to: Dark, Bleed
  • Immune To: Poison/Toxic
